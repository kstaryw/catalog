Why do so many business strategies fail? Introduction to system dynamics modeling applied
to corporate strategy. Uses a mixture of simulation models, role-playing games, and case
studies to develop principles for successful
management of complex strategies in a
dynamic world. Case studies of successful
strategy design using system dynamics. Considers strategic issues such as business
cycles, market growth and stagnation, the diffusion of new technologies, the misuse of
forecasts, and the rationality of managerial
decision making.
